## Conclusions (metrics — confirm by ear)

### Root cause

1. **Hiss is quantization noise**, not v5 entropy or v5.1 band modes. High v4 / v5 / v5.1 share the same quant path on flat frames; v5 pack roundtrip is lossless.
2. **Full-song SNR hid the problem.** At the old High step (0.018), quiet-passage SNR was ~0.7 dB. After retune (0.014 + stronger quiet scaling): **2.6 dB** quiet SNR, **38.6 dB** full SNR.
3. **Worst sections:** outro, reverb tails, intro gaps. HF spectral error ~2.4% (High) vs ~1.6% (Extreme).
4. **encode_v4_reference** now writes `0x43 0x04`. Old listening files named `_v4` were v5.1 — re-export for A/B.

### Default preset

**Extreme** for listening (converter default). **High** retuned — ear-test before restoring as default. See `docs/MP5C_HISS_INVESTIGATION.md`.
